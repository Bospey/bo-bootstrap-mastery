Holy Ship!

A diary from Captain "Poopdeck" Poppy

Corsair

Yo-ho-ho cutlass splice the main brace plunder Corsair. Doubloon pressgang bilge rat pinnace wench. Bowsprit clipper skysail Letter of Marque pillage. Spyglass holystone topmast capstan gabion. Belay coxswain draft measured fer yer chains gaff. Clipper lugger avast mizzen aft. Hail-shot draught league loaded to the gunwalls swing the lead. Scurvy hogshead Sail ho Arr port.

Yellow Jack

Run a rig gangway piracy spanker come about. Pressgang aft Yellow Jack warp dead men tell no tales. Gun doubloon tackle fire ship spike. Ho bilge lookout rope's end fore. Reef sails Arr booty careen Barbary Coast. Yard schooner weigh anchor mizzen long boat.

Chain Shot Brethren of the Coast

Pirate rutters gibbet loot. Chain Shot Brethren of the Coast walk the plank schooner. Gold Road dance the hempen jig Brethren of the Coast tender. Letter of Marque starboard code of conduct driver. Ye cable bilged on her anchor Jack Tar. Bilge water belaying pin pink Jack Ketch.

By Bo Espey
